Services

Gangotri Art Studio offers a wide range of photography services, including:

Wedding Photography

The team at Gangotri Art Studio specializes in capturing stunning and timeless photographs of weddings and other special events. We use a blend of traditional and candid photography styles to showcase the beauty and emotion of each moment.

Portrait Photography

We offers a variety of portrait photography services, including family portraits, individual portraits, and business headshots. The photographers are skilled at capturing natural and authentic expressions to create beautiful and memorable photographs.

Event Photography

We provides professional photography services for a wide range of events, including corporate events, parties, and celebrations. We use our expertise to capture the highlights and special moments of each event.

Video Production

In addition to photography, We also offers video production services. We can help with everything from creating promotional videos to documenting events.
